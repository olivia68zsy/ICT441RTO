4 School Values

STC Unique Advantages

  • Focus on innovative education

    Superstar Technology Colleges prioritize cultivating students' innovation and problem-solving abilities. They offer courses and hands-on opportunities that cover the latest technologies and knowledge, as well as interdisciplinary education.

  • Industry-academia collaboration

    Superstar Technology Colleges closely collaborate with businesses and enterprises to provide students with real-world career experiences and practical opportunities. Students can participate in actual projects, learn and apply practical skills and knowledge.

  • International perspective

    Superstar Technology Colleges have broad international cooperative partners and student groups, providing various exchange and learning opportunities. Students can gain a global perspective and cultural experience, enhance cross-cultural communication and leadership skills.

  • Entrepreneurship incubator

    Superstar Technology Colleges encourage students to start their own businesses and provide various resources and support. Students can obtain guidance, funding, and training for start-ups, thus turning their ideas into reality and achieving their dreams.
